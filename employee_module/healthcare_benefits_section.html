Group Health Insurance
Terms and Conditions


Inclusions Exclusions



COVID-19 treatment

Covers COVID-19 treatment if test results are positive and the patient has been hospitalized for more than 24 hours


Hospitalization

Covers expenses upto ₹5L for hospitalization due to disease /illness/ injury during the policy period that requires an insured person's admission in a hospital as an inpatient. Medical expenses directly related to the hospitalization would be payable


Shared coverage

You, your spouse and your children can claim a maximum combined amount of ₹5,00,000 per year


Day Care Treatments

Covers expenses for 540+ day care treatment due to disease/ illness/ injury during the policy period taken at a hospital or a day care centre


Room Rent Limit

Maximum limit on room rent charges for Normal rooms is ₹5,000 per day and for ICU rooms is ₹10,000 per day.

In case the room rent exceeds this amount, the differential amount and additional cost of services will be borne by the policyholder proportionately


Maternity

Maternity costs upto ₹50,000 on normal delivery and upto ₹50,000 on caesarean delivery. Pre and post natal expenses within maternity limit


Pre-existing Disease

Pre-existing diseases are covered with no waiting period


Pre and Post Hospitalisation

Medical expenses incurred 60 days before admission and 90 days after discharge from hospital.


Lasik Surgery

Lasik Surgery is covered if correction index is +/- 6.5D or more


Mental Illness

Treatment of mental illness upto ₹30,000


In-patient Dental Treatment

Covers expenses incurred towards hospitalization for dental treatment under anesthesia necessitated due to an accident/ injury/ illness


Homecare treatment for COVID-19

Covered on prior approval from the insurer for reimbursement claims. Cashless coverage also requires intimation.


Ambulance

Covers expenses incurred on transportation of Insured Person in a registered ambulance to a hospital in case of an emergency or from one hospital to another, for better medical facilities & treatment, subject to 3000 per Hospitalization.


AYUSH benefits

Covers medical expenses incurred for In-patient treatment taken under Ayurveda, Unani, Sidha or Homeopathy.


Domiciliary Treatment

Covers medical expenses incurred for availing medical treatment at home which would otherwise have required hospitalization. We will also cover pre and post hospitalization expenses in case of domiciliary hospitalization


Organ Donor

Covers Medical and surgical Expenses of the organ donor for harvesting the organ where an Insured Person is the recipient


Consumable Benefits

Covers expenses incurred, for specified consumables, which are consumed during the period of hospitalization directly related to the insured person’s medical or surgical treatment of illness/disease/injury


Health Check-up

Expenses for a Preventive Health Check-up upto 1% of previous year policy sum insured subject to a maximum of 310,000/- per policy once in block of every two continuous daim free policy years with us
